About Myself

I'm a computer scientist and entrepreneur, who is passionate about philosophy and its implications for our everyday lives. During my studies at ETH Zurich, I developed the Digital ID protocol with the goal of replacing closed platforms with open standards. I then co-founded Synacts, which provides an open-source implementation of this protocol and helps its clients build secure services. I recently joined Shift Cryptosecurity as a cryptography specialist and software engineer to enhance the Digital Bitbox.
